About GreenStream

Founded to address the growing problem of electronic waste in urban and rural communities, GreenStream partners with local schools, businesses, and municipalities to offer safe collection and recycling services.

Our programs

  • Collection Drives: Neighborhood drop-off points and scheduled pickups.
  • Certified Recycling: Partnering with licensed recyclers to recover materials safely.
  • Education & Training: Workshops on safe disposal and device refurbishment.

Impact

Since 2023 we've collected over 20 tonnes of e-waste and trained community members in safe handling and repair skills.
